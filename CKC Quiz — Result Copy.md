### Ruby — The Queen of Momentum
*Core energy: Momentum | Known for: Arriving first*

You were never built for waiting.
You move before certainty arrives. Somehow, you're usually right.
*The Ruby Queen arrives first. She always has.*

**Others experience you as:** impossible to slow down.

---

### Emerald — The Queen of Desire
*Core energy: Desire | Known for: Getting exactly what she wants*

You want things deeply, and without apology.
You're patient not because you doubt yourself, but because you already know it's coming.
*The Emerald Queen has never confused desire with impossibility.*

**Others experience you as:** impossible to rush.

---

### Opal — The Queen of Reinvention
*Core energy: Reinvention | Known for: Becoming someone new*

You've outgrown versions of yourself that other people are still trying to reach.
No one has ever fully figured you out — including you, sometimes. That's the point.
*The Opal Queen becomes someone new. She always has somewhere else to be.*

**Others experience you as:** impossible to predict.

---

### Pearl — The Queen of Knowing
*Core energy: Knowing | Known for: Already knowing*

You already knew. Before it was said. Before it happened. Before it made sense.
You carry things quietly — the ones no one else noticed, the ones no one asked about.
*The Pearl Queen already knew.*

**Others experience you as:** impossible to read.

---

### Citrine — The Queen of Recognition
*Core energy: Recognition | Known for: Being unforgettable*

You take up exactly as much space as you're meant to.
Being overlooked was never going to happen to you twice.
*The Citrine Queen is unforgettable. She made sure of it.*

**Others experience you as:** impossible to forget.

---

### Peridot — The Queen of Precision
*Core energy: Precision | Known for: Being correct*

You see what's off before anyone else does — and it bothers you until it's right.
You're not being critical. You're being accurate.
*The Peridot Queen is correct. Notoriously.*

**Others experience you as:** impossible to satisfy.

---

### Rose Quartz — The Queen of Devotion
*Core energy: Devotion | Known for: Loving deliberately*

You love with intention — which means you've learned exactly who deserves it.
Your softness is not weakness. It's a very specific kind of power.
*The Rose Quartz Queen loves deliberately. Not everyone gets chosen.*

**Others experience you as:** impossibly warm, until you're not.

---

### Obsidian — The Queen of Control
*Core energy: Control | Known for: Remaining unreadable*

People mistake your stillness for absence.
Your silence isn't distance — it's patience with a plan attached.
*The Obsidian Queen remains unreadable. That was always the point.*

**Others experience you as:** emotionally unreadable.

---

### Sapphire — The Queen of Exploration
*Core energy: Exploration | Known for: Leaving*

You've never been able to pretend a ceiling isn't there.
Honesty gets you in trouble sometimes. You've decided it's worth it.
*The Sapphire Queen leaves. She was always going to.*

**Others experience you as:** impossible to keep.

---

### Garnet — The Queen of Vigilance
*Core energy: Vigilance | Known for: Watching quietly*

You've been watching longer than anyone realizes. Building longer too.
When you finally move, it will look sudden to everyone except you.
*The Garnet Queen has been watching. She already knows how this ends.*

**Others experience you as:** quietly formidable.

---

### Aquamarine — The Queen of Vision
*Core energy: Vision | Known for: Seeing further*

It already exists somewhere. You just got there first.
You care — you just sometimes forget to show up in the ways people expect.
*The Aquamarine Queen sees further. She stopped explaining what she sees.*

**Others experience you as:** slightly ahead of reality.

---

### Moonstone — The Queen of Dissolution
*Core energy: Dissolution | Known for: Dissolving boundaries*

You absorb things — rooms, moods, people — without meaning to.
The line between you and the world is thinner than it is for most. You've learned to work with that.
*The Moonstone Queen dissolves the distance. She felt you long before you arrived.*

**Others experience you as:** strangely comforting in ways they can't explain.
