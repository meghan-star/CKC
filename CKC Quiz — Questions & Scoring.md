# CKC Gem Familiar Quiz — Questions & Scoring Map
## "Find Your Familiar" | 8 Questions | 12 Outcomes

Each answer maps to 3 queens (1 point each). Highest total after 8 questions = your familiar.
Q8 weighted 3 pts — tiebreaker, designed to split Opal/Sapphire (closest pair).
Secondary influence: if 2nd-place queen is within 1 point of top, display both.
Example: "You are **Sapphire** — with **Opal** influence."

## Notes for the Build

**Scoring:** Max possible 10 pts per queen (7 regular + 3 for Q8). Typical winner: 2–4 pts.

**Secondary influence:** Display if 2nd queen is within 1 point of top. Use identity language:
- *"You are [Queen] — with [Queen] influence."*
- Result copy can acknowledge the blend (see V2 notes below).

**Tiebreaker:** If top two queens are tied after Q8, primary = whoever scored Q8. Q8 was designed for this.

**Closest pair:** Opal and Sapphire share 6/8 answer slots. Q3 (D vs B) and Q8 (C×3 vs A×3) are the only separators. The 3x Q8 weight handles it.

**Result reveal mechanic:** Answers collected silently. On complete, face-down tarot fan animates → one card flips → result screen. The "draw" feels mystical; the scoring runs behind the scenes.

**Result copy tone:** Prophetic, slightly dangerous, deeply specific. Never generic.
- ✗ "You are bold and adventurous."
- ✓ "You were never built for stillness."
- ✓ "You notice things other people survive by ignoring."
- ✓ "You became difficult to categorize on purpose."
