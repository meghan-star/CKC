## Generation Notes

**If ChatGPT still creates a glass/crystal cat:** Add to the end of the prompt — "The cat has realistic natural fur. The crystals are individual gems set INTO the fur, not the material of the fur itself. Think of diamonds set in velvet fabric, not a glass sculpture."

**If gem crystals aren't prominent enough:** Add "each gem individually rendered as a distinct faceted stone with visible internal light — not a shimmer or sheen, actual separate crystals."

**If the crown is wrong:** Name the crown type explicitly again in a follow-up: "The tiara should be [type] — [brief description]. Please regenerate with that specific crown silhouette."

**Recommended generation order:** Start with whichever feels most exciting. High enthusiasm = better results. Obsidian and Garnet tend to generate cleanly due to strong contrast. Opal and Moonstone can need extra prompting to get the iridescence right.
